FOOD WASTE FACT

DID U KNOW

Did you know that approximately one-third of all food produced worldwide for human consumption is either lost or wasted every year? This amounts to about 1.3 billion tons of food wasted annually. Reducing food waste is crucial for sustainable food systems and addressing global hunger and environmental challenges.

FOOD MANAGEMENT

FIVE STEPS

Sure! Here are five steps for food management:

  1. Plan purchases to avoid overbuying.
  2. Store food properly to maintain freshness.
  3. Use FIFO rotation to prioritize older items.
  4. Portion control to minimize leftovers.
  5. Compost or donate excess food to reduce waste.
